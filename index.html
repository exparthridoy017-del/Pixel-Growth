About Me

Design with intent. Marketing with data.

I'm Hridoy Ray, a digital marketing expert and graphic designer based in Comilla, Chattogram, Bangladesh. Over the past few years I've helped local restaurants, product brands, and startups turn plain ideas into scroll-stopping visuals and campaigns that actually convert — from Facebook ad creatives and SEO-optimized content to full brand identities and lead-generation funnels.

My approach sits at the intersection of design and performance: every visual I ship is built to look premium and earn its place in a growth strategy.
